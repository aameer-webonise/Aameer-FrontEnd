Closing and Self Closing Tags

Self Closing tags

A self-closing tag is a special kind of HTML tag that does not have a closing tag counterpart.
The tags that aren’t required to have specific closing tags are called “self closing” tags.
The most known self closing tags are:

  1. <br>
  2. <hr>
  3. <img>
  4. <input>
  5. <link>
  6. <meta>
  7. <keygen>
  8. <command>
  9. <param>
  10. <source>
  11. <wbr>

Closing tags

The tags that required to have specific closing tags are called "closing tags". The most known closing tags are:

  1. <html>..contents..</html>
  2. <head>..contents..</head>
  3. <body>..contents..</body>
  4. <ol>..contents..</ol>
  5. <ul>..contents..</ul>
  6. <dl>..contents..</dl>
  7. <li>..contents..</li>
  8. <p>..contents..</p>
  9. <dt>..contents..</dt>
  10. <dd>..contents..</dd>
  11. <table>..contents..</table>
  12. <th>..contents..</th>
  13. <tr>..contents..</tr>
  14. <td>..contents..</td>
